Tips to Park your Vehicle

Use your signals:

Before you pull into a parking spot, use your turn signal to indicate your intention to other drivers.

Check your surroundings:

Look around to make sure that there are no pedestrians, cyclists, or obstacles in your way.

Position your vehicle:

Position your vehicle in the center of the parking space, ensuring that there is enough space between your vehicle and the adjacent vehicles.

Check your distance:

VMake sure that you have enough distance between your vehicle and the vehicles in front and behind you. This will prevent you from accidentally hitting them when you pull out of the parking space.

Set the parking brake:

Engage your parking brake to ensure that your vehicle does not move while parked.
